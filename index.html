Our Services

Investment Decisions

Investment Management

We invest on behalf of our clients and provide continuous monitoring of your investment portfolio with expert advice.

This option is for those who want to invest under their names but needs the help of the firm in monitoring the investment portfolio, advising on when to reallocate the funds, giving feedback on the developments on their investments and the developments in the investment industry. Our team provides regular performance reports and adjusts your portfolio based on changing market conditions and your evolving financial goals.

Investment Advisory

Tailored financial advice and investment recommendations to help you grow and protect your wealth effectively.

We provide financial advice and make investment recommendations to our clients, primarily focusing on investments and wealth management, helping clients manage and grow their wealth. We develop tailored investment strategies based on individual financial goals, such as retirement, education savings or long-term growth. We specialise in helping clients build tailored investment plans designed to strategically grow and protect your wealth. By working closely with our clients, we analyse your financial goals and timelines to craft personalized strategies.

Investment Fund

We pool money from multiple investors to purchase diversified assets, aiming to generate optimal returns for our clients.

This applies to those who want the firm to invest their money on their behalf. We pool money from multiple investors to purchase a diversified portfolio of assets. We oversee these investments, with the aim of generating best returns for our clients. We invest in Government securities, in fast growing profitable private companies, stock exchange and other relevant investments. Under this option, you benefit from the growth in the valuation of the investments at the point of exit from the fund and or from the regular profit sharing.

Wealth Management

Comprehensive services including tax planning, estate planning, retirement planning, and more to maximize your net worth.

We help maximise your net worth. This involves managing investments or assets portfolios along with tax planning, managing education planning, estate planning, legacy planning, insurance, retirement planning. Our holistic approach ensures all aspects of your financial life work together to achieve your long-term objectives while minimizing tax liabilities and protecting your assets for future generations.

Equity Investment

Become a shareholder of Nadis Capital and benefit from capital gains and dividend payments as our company grows.

You can become a shareholder of Nadis Capital by buying the shares of Nadis Capital. Under this option, you benefit from the capital gains resulting from the growth in the value of Nadis Capital shares, at the time of disposal of your shares as well as the dividend payments. As a shareholder, you'll also have access to exclusive investor updates and opportunities to participate in special investment vehicles.
